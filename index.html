About novosta

novosta is a lightweight digital label and artist support system for underground, independent musicians. We don’t claim ownership of your music. Our goal is to keep things legally safe and smoothly distributed without any corporate overhead or creative restrictions.

We primarily assist with metadata formatting, release management, copyright disputes, and distro communication. Our mission is to let you focus on the art, while we handle the boring backend stuff.
