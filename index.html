novosta is a digital-first label focused on electronic sound — from ambient to club-ready. We prioritize artist freedom, clean visuals, and long-term support.

about the label

founded with a focus on detail and longevity, novosta represents a new generation of electronic artists. we collaborate with producers across genres like techno, experimental, ambient, and alternative electronic. each release is fully supported — from metadata to DSP strategy — while keeping artistic integrity front and center.

we’re not just a distro. we’re a platform for artists who care deeply about sound, aesthetic, and control.
